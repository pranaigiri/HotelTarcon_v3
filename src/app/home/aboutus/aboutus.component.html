Welcome to Hotel Tarcon

Experience a contemporary hotel with premium bar & authentic restaurant offering excitement for every visitor! Hotel Tarcon is located within walking distance from MG Marg and is the one of the best hotel in the heart of Gangtok with private parking on site, majestic hills views, natural breeze that makes the soul happy.

The hotel, furthermore, is provided with amazing rooms that maintained the style and the classic ambiance of a historic dwelling, the ideal choice for a holiday in the sign of refinement.

Our bar & restaurant, will entice you with a deliciously refreshed foods, traditional menu and traditional wines comprised of the finest hand-selected produce from the organic farms.
